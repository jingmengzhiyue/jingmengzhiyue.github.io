Greetings, my name is Huoshuai, and I have obtained a Master’s degree in Signal and Information Processing. My primary research is anchored in the domain of remote sensing image processing, specifically employing optimization theories to the fusion of hyperspectral and multispectral images. Additionally, my scholarly interests encompass computer vision within the paradigms of machine learning and deep learning. I am also intrigued by the exploration of natural language processing and deep reinforcement learning, with a dedicated focus on the interpretability of deep learning models. Currently, I serve as an Algorithm Engineer at the Chongqing Western Handwriting Big Data Research Institute, where I am responsible for the pen sequence restoration project.

Through my studies and research, I have developed a strong understanding of the latest techniques and technologies in these fields. I am constantly striving to learn more and expand my knowledge, and I am excited to see how my work can contribute to the advancement of these areas.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me a bit better. I look forward to exploring these topics further and working with others who share my passion for these fascinating fields.
